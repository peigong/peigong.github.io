# Q&A: The temple of heaven

72. What was the original name of the Temple of Heaven? And what was the main construction called?

It was first called Temple of Heaven and Earth. And the main construction was called Hall of Great Sacrifice.

73. Which emperor in the Ming Dynasty converted the rule of worshipping to the Heaven and the Earth together into doing it respectively?

Emperor Jiajing.

74. During the ceremony, who will work on the slab paved path to the western side of the Red Stairway(Danbi) Bridge?

It was reserved for the officials who accompanied the emperor for ceremony.

75. Which altar does the Hall of Imperial Zenith belong to as the so called 'Heavenly Storehouse'?

It belongs to the Altar of Prayer for Grain.

76. How old was the emperor allowed to enter the door on the western corner of the Hall of Imperial Zenith according to the Qing rulers?

70 years old.

77. What is the function of the Long Corridor to the eastern side of the Hall of Prayer for Good Harvest?

This is a special path for the sacrificial of offerings, and the corridor gives a shelter that may keep the wind and dust away.

78. When did the emperors do the worshiping at the Circular Mound of Altar in the Ming and Qing Dynasties? When did it start?

The worshipping day is on the day of Winter Solstice. And the ceremony usually started at 4:15 am to 4:30 am.

79. How many emperors have come to Temple of Heaven to do the worship in Ming and Qing Dynasties? And how many times did the worships get done?

22 emperors and 654 times.

80. Where did the grain praying take place in the Temple of Heaven?

It took place at the Altar of Prayer for Grain.

81. Where was the worshipping place for the emperor during the ceremony?

The emperor's worshipping position was due south on the second tier of the Altar.

82. Along the walkway in front of the Imperial Vault of Heaven, from north to south, what is the 3rd stone slab called?

It is called Triple Sound Stone.

83. What is the diameter of the second tier on the Circular Mound of Altar? What is the total number of that for all three tiers and what are the multiples of the number of all carved balusters on each tier?

It's 15 Zhang. And the total number is 45 Zhang. They are multiples of number nine.

84. When was the Divine Music Administration firstly built? And when was the name changed to the current name?

It was called the Temple of Divine Music at the beginning. And its name was changed in Qianlong reign in Qing Dynasty.

85. What does the plaque, written by emperor, hung in the Divine Music administration say?

Yuzhenjinsheng, it means the chime and bell ring, and it was written by Qianlong to sing high praise for harmonious melody and sound.

86. Who were the performers that did all ceremonial practice in the early Ming Dynasty at the Divine Music Administration

They were Taoists.

87. What is the glazed-tiles color on the roof of the Imperial Vault of Heaven?

Blue.

88. What is the height of the Hall of Prayer for Good Harvest on the Altar of Prayer for Grain?

It is 38 meters with its diameter around 32 meters.

89. What is the round shape slab called in the middle of the the Circular Mound of Altar?

It is called Heavenly Heart Stone.

90. What are the two walls in and out of the Circular Mound of Altar called? What are the shapes of them?

The round one is calledd Inner Wall, and the square one is called Outer Wall.

91. The Echo Wall is the inner wall of the circular courtyard wall of which building in the Temple of Heaven? What is the cause of its acoustic phenomenon?

Imperial Vault of Heaven. There are three reasons: one is that the wall surrounds the yard into a circle, and the sound is reflected when it hits the wall; the other is that the wall is very smooth, and the sound lose when hitting the wall is quite small; and the 3rd is that the tiles on the wall are inward that increases the effect of gathering sounds.

93. What type of pattern was engraved on the Seven Star Stones?

The mountain patterns.

94. Where is the additional stone added to the original Seven Star Stones? Which mountain does the additional one represent? What does it mean?

The additional one is added to the northeastern part versus the original Seven Star Stones. It represents Changbai Mountain. It means union of Manchu and Han, and the union of the country.

95. What is the main hall called in the Palace of Abstinence? What is the pavilion to the south called in front of the main hall? What is the pavilion to the north called in front of the main hall?

It is called Beamless Hall. It is called Time Table Pavilion. It is called Pavilion of Bronze Figure.

96. During the ceremony, what is the total height of the Lanterns hung on the Lantern Post in the Circular Mound of Altar? What the candle called? How long does this candle burn?

It's about 2 meters. Overnight candles with dragon patterns. It may last for about six hours.

97. Which direction does the main building in the Hall of Abstinence face?

It sits west and faces east.

98. What is the roof called of the Ningxi Hall in the Divine Music Administration?

It is called hipped roof with single eave.

99. What sacred tablets are enshrined in the main hall and the east and west side halls of the Imperial Vault of Heaven?

The main hall: the God of Heaven and the sacred tablets of the first eight emperors of the Qing Dynasty. The eastern hall: the God of Sunlight and the Gods of different stars. The western hall: the God of Moonlight and the Gods of Wind, Cloud, Rain and Thunder.

100. What characters are used on the sacred tablet in the Imperial Vault of Heaven?

Manchurain And Chinese characters.

101. Who were the emperors that the first built, renovated, expanded,and then brought the Temple of Heaven to its heyday

Emperor Yongle first built it. Emperor Jiajing renovated that, and Emperor Qianlong expanded and brought it to its Heyday.

102. The Echo Wall is one of the four great acoustic buildings in ancient China. Where are the other three buildings located?

Henan Baolun Temple Pagoda(commonly known as Toad Pagoda); Sichuan(Tongnan Country Grand Buddha Temple) The Harp Sound of Stone Stairway;Shanxi Pujiu Temple Pagoda(also known as Yingying Pagoda)

103. What are these three slab paths called on the Red Stairway Bridge(Danbi Bridge)?

Middle one is reserved for god, called the Divine Path. The east one is for emperor called Imperial Path. And the west one is for the official and royal family members called the Ministerial Path.

104. What is the function of the Butcher Pavilion?

It is the place where the calves and sacrificed animals get slaughtered before the sacrifice.

105. What is the function of the main hall in the Divine Music Administration?

It is the place where are those who are in charge of music, dance and ceremony issues study and practice.
